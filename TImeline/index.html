Digital Freedom Timeline: India

Independence

India gains independence from British rule, marking the beginning of a new era of self-governance and nation-building.

Aryabhata Satellite

India launches its first satellite, Aryabhatta, a major step towards strengthening its scientific and technological capabilities.

IT Boom

The rise of the Indian IT sector transforms the nation into a global hub for software development and technology services.

UPI Launch

The Unified Payments Interface (UPI) is launched, revolutionizing digital payments and financial inclusion for millions of citizens.

Digital India, AI

The 'Digital India' initiative drives a nationwide digital transformation, with a strong focus on emerging technologies like AI.

Vision 2047

Envisioning a future where India is a global leader in technology, innovation, and digital governance.
